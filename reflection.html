📚 Resilience Bookstore Corner

🕌 In the Footsteps of the Prophet – Tariq Ramadan

Genre: Islamic Spirituality / Biography
Why it's powerful: A gentle yet profound guide to the life and wisdom of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). This book offers peace and reflection to those navigating hardship. For the incarcerated, it inspires patience, compassion, and hope rooted in faith—reminding them they are never alone.

🕵️‍♀️ The Housemaid’s Secret – Freida McFadden

Genre: Psychological Thriller / Mystery
Why it's powerful: A gripping narrative filled with secrets and suspense. This story resonates with those feeling trapped—whether physically or emotionally. It reflects on what it means to protect yourself, navigate deceit, and seek justice even in silence.

🌹 The Rose That Grew from Concrete – Tupac Shakur

Genre: Poetry / Autobiographical Reflections
Why it's powerful: Tupac’s poetry captures struggle, strength, and soul. For anyone locked up or left out, his words become a mirror and a battle cry. The rose that broke through the concrete becomes a symbol of resilience—just like them.
